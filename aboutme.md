My older brother studies computer science and my mother is a hair braider and sewer.  When I moved away from college, I was stunned by how useful their skills were and my dependence on them from small, everyday help to large academic or personal projects.  I decided in my  final semester, I should empower myself. Who knows when I’ll move away next? How far will I go and for how long? It is best to initiate the steps in a low-stakes environment, in a class full of beginners.
