homemade switches, ya heard? How??...well a circut w/ 1 LED (used a yellow one) & some conductive thread, fabric, & a paperclip. 


tip & trick as we dooo, nothing new. 

* if your using conductive fabric cut it into smaller pieces for easier use/sewing
* consider the distance between your materials, if you don't need hella thread, don't waste your time pullling elbow length / sensitive thread for 3 stiches ... its weird  


  


peace & love,
